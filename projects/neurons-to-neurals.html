From Neurons to Neural Networks

Jina Nicholas, Izzy Trudel

*****

April 2026



CSB198 was a seminar (small class size) course, exploring cell and molecular biology in current research and news. The course emphasized a lot of experiential learning.

At the beginning of the course, I expected it to be relatively straightforward, as I was taking it to fill a breadth requirement only. I assumed that the content would be less demanding and more focused on just general knowledge. However, it was a lot more techincal, but I was pleasantly surprised by how much I enjoyed it!

All in all, for me, this course changed biology from something I once avoided into a field I now see as relevant, interesting, and full of opportunities to connect with my future in computer science.
